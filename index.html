  • DPSSL started 7 clubs

    The seven clubs are :

    1. TINKERING CLUB (STEAM FUN)-"The club visions to build scientific temperament, curiosity, creativity and imagination in young minds. It would develop skills such as design mind-set, computational thinking through hands-on activities." 2. THE MASK (ENGLISH CLUB)-"The club aims to trigger the learning graph of students and elevate their knowledge and ingenuity by giving them a perfect platform to strengthen their linguistic and theatrical skills through myriad activities. It would give an opportunity to its members to display their intellectual and independent thinking skills through public speaking and drama." 3. INDRADHANUSH (HINDI CLUB) -"The club gives a perfect exposure to its students to hone their literary and theatrical skills in Hindi language through a series of fun-filled activities. It aims to help the students to overcome their fear for the language and make them confident public speakers, presenters, and performers." 4. NOTES AND BEATZ (MUSIC CLUB)-"The club aims to develop a rich array of musical skills among students. They will be introduced to various musical instruments and get an opportunity to showcase their talent in various events. The club also focuses on training and practising classical and western vocals." 5. The club focuses on agility, strengthening concentration power, hand-eye as well as neuro-muscular coordination of the body to improve fitness levels. Members of the club would enjoy challenging fun-filled games like flip the bottle, around the world, cookie killer etc. 6. EMPASSION (SOCIAL SERVICE CLUB)-"The club aims to sensitize students by giving them an opportunity to contribute to the well-being of others and the environment. The students would be encouraged to participate in various donation drives and altruistic activities like Food-a-thon, visit to old age homes and so on. " 7. PRAKRITI (ECO CLUB)-"The main focus of the club is to develop environmental consciousness amongst learners and to make them eco- warriors who can motivate their family and friends to adopt the environment friendly ways. The club endeavours to prepare young guardians of the land who work towards its conservation and sustainability. "
